Ice Cream

Description

Ice cream is a classic treat that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, enjoying a summer day, or just craving something sweet, ice cream is the way to go. With so many flavors to choose from, you can never go wrong. From classic vanilla and chocolate to unique flavors like mint chocolate chip and rocky road, there's something for everyone. Top your scoops with sprinkles, whipped cream, or a cherry on top for an extra special treat!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flavorings (Chocolate, Mint, etc.)
  • Optional Toppings (Sprinkles, Whipped Cream, Cherry)

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients: milk, cream, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  2. Combine the milk, cream, and sugar in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Stir the mixture until the sugar is dissolved and the liquid is heated through.
  4. Remove the mixture from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Let the mixture cool, then transfer it to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  6. Once the ice cream is churned, transfer it to a container and freeze until firm.
  7. Scoop your ice cream into cones or bowls, add your favorite toppings, and enjoy!
