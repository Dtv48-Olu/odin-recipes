Pancakes

Description

Making pancakes is a breeze! Just whisk together your dry ingredients like flour, sugar, and baking powder. In a separate bowl, combine the wet ingredients like milk, egg, and melted butter. Gently mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just combined - a few lumps are okay! Then, heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat and pour in your batter, a Â¼ cup at a time. Let the pancakes cook until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges look dry. Flip and cook for another minute or two, until golden brown. Once done, stack those beauties high and drizzle with your favorite syrup for a delicious breakfast!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather your dry ingredients: 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons of sugar, and 2 teaspoons of baking powder.
  2. Don't forget the wet stuff: In a separate bowl, whisk together 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of melted butter (or vegetable oil).
  3. Pantry staples: You'll also need a pinch of salt and some vegetable oil for cooking.
  4. Sweet tooth satisfied (optional): Grab your favorite syrup for topping the pancakes!
