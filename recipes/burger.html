Burger

Description

Who doesn't love a good burger? Start with a juicy beef patty, cooked to perfection. Add your favorite cheese, like cheddar or Swiss, and melt it over the patty while it's still hot. Place the patty on a toasted bun and top with fresh lettuce, tomato, and any other toppings you desire. Don't forget the condiments - ketchup, mustard, and mayo are classic choices. Serve with a side of crispy fries or a fresh salad for a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Beef Patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Burger Bun
  • Condiments (Ketchup, Mustard, Mayo)

Steps

  1. Start with a fresh beef patty - you can season it with salt and pepper for extra flavor.
  2. Cook the patty on a hot grill or skillet until it reaches your desired level of doneness.
  3. While the patty is still hot, add a slice of your favorite cheese on top to melt.
  4. Toast the burger bun until golden brown and assemble your burger with the patty, cheese, lettuce, and tomato.
  5. Don't forget the condiments - spread ketchup, mustard, and mayo on the bun for extra flavor.
  6. Serve your delicious burger with a side of crispy fries or a fresh salad for a complete meal!
